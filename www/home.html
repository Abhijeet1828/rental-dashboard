Whether you’re an international student who’s new to Melbourne, or a seasoned domestic student looking for a new place, we know house hunting can be daunting.
On this platform, we provide you with information about Accommodation, Public Transport, Environment and Points of Interests to help you enjoy your new life in Melbourne! You can find some information below and for more details please refer to menu.








Apartment Hunting

The very first thing that comes to students mind when they decide to start studying in another country or city is having an appropriate place as their accommodation. In their point of view, this is the most challenging part of starting their new journey as a university student.
We have good news for you!
Through this platform, you can easily search for your intended accommodations, compare them with each other and choose the best one which suits you.






Places of Interest

Starting your housing journey as a student isn't just about finding the right price . It's also about finding a favourite stomping ground and a reliably priced gym! Use our interface to explore the area around your prospective new home and find the best doctors, coffee, coworking spaces and anything else you need.
